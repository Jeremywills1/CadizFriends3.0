About me

I was not raised in a church, but I came to accept Jesus as Savior and Lord during my freshman year of college. My Christian life has been filled with highs and lows, but the Lord always had a calling on my heart. In December 2011, at age 56, I accepted that call and became the pastor at Kennard Friends Church. In May 2016 I started pastoring a second congregation, at the Cadiz Friends Church. My messages are simple and focused on connecting with people and connecting people with Christ. I believe in teaching the Bible in a way that provides information, inspiration and application. Or, to say it another way... taking the Bible from head (thinking) to heart (feeling) to hand (doing).
